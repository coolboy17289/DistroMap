## Summary

Nobara Linux is a Linux distribution based on Fedora Linux. It is developed by Thomas Crider, otherwise more colloquially referred to as "Glorious Eggroll", who is also known for developing Proton-GE, a modification of Valve Corporation's compatibility layer, widely used for running Windows games on Linux.
